## 📸 How to Add Images (3 Simple Steps)

### 1. Get Your Image URL

**Option A: Free Hosting (Recommended)**
- Upload to [ImgBB.com](https://imgbb.com/) (free, no account)
- Copy the "Direct link"

**Option B: Use Your Own**
- Upload to your website/server
- Use full HTTPS URL

### 2. Add to Admin Panel

```
Admin Panel → Products → Edit/Add Product
└─ Image URL field → Paste your link
   └─ Example: https://i.ibb.co/abc123/product.jpg
```

### 3. Save & View

Your product now has a professional image on the storefront!

## 🎨 Image Best Practices

### Recommended Specs
- **Size:** 800x800px (square)
- **Format:** JPG or PNG
- **File size:** Under 200KB
- **Background:** White or neutral

### Quality Tips
✅ Well-lit, clear photos
✅ Show product from best angle
✅ Consistent style across all products
✅ No watermarks or text overlays

## 🔍 Troubleshooting

**Image not showing?**
- Ensure URL ends in `.jpg` or `.png` (direct link, not webpage)
- Test URL in browser - should show only image
- Use HTTPS, not HTTP

**Image looks stretched?**
- Use square images (1:1 ratio) for best results

**Slow loading?**
- Compress images at [TinyPNG.com](https://tinypng.com)
